Recipe

Cabbage 150g
Flour 50g
Tapioca Flour / Corn Starch 20g
Large Eggs 2
Water 2 Tbs

Seasoning:
Ground Ginger 1 Tsp
Worcestershire Sauce 1 Tsp
Soy Sauce 2 Tsp

Toppings:
Scallops 4
Prawns 5-8
Smoked Salmon 2 Slices

To Finish:
Okonomiyaki Sauce
Mayonnaise
Dried Seaweed / Dried Parsley


method

Finely slice the cabbage and transfer it to a mixing bowl.

To prepare the seafood, slice the scallops in half and cut the smoked salmon into small pieces. (Feel free to use any protein of your choice, ensuring it’s cut into bite-sized pieces.)

In a separate bowl, beat two eggs and then add the flour and cornstarch. Mix until smooth.

Add the egg-flour mixture to the cabbage and season with the seasoning ingredients. Mix well to combine.

Preheat a stainless steel pan over medium heat until hot. (Tip: Test the heat by dropping a bead of water into the pan—if the water glides, the pan is ready.)

Add 1 tablespoon of oil to the pan, ensuring it coats the surface.

Pour the cabbage mixture into the pan, then add a few drops of water. Cover with a lid and steam-fry for 5 minutes.

Remove the lid and add the scallops and prawns to the side of the pancake, frying until golden.

Once the seafood is cooked, add it onto the pancake. Flip the pancake using two turners, add a dash of water, and steam for another 5 minutes.

Transfer the pancake to a plate, then drizzle with okonomiyaki sauce and mayonnaise. Use a fork to create a desired pattern, and sprinkle dried seaweed on top.
